Sri Krishna High School


Sri Krishna High School is a well-established institution dedicated to providing quality education up to Class X. The school focuses on nurturing young minds through a balanced approach that combines academic excellence with character development. With a structured curriculum, experienced faculty, and modern teaching methodologies, the school ensures that every student receives the right guidance to excel in studies as well as in life.


The environment at Sri Krishna High School is designed to encourage curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. Classrooms are equipped with effective learning tools, and teachers pay individual attention to students to help them overcome challenges and achieve their full potential. Alongside academics, the school emphasizes extracurricular activities, ensuring that children grow holistically with confidence and discipline.

About us

The school follows clear rules to maintain punctuality, regular attendance, respect for teachers, and good behavior, ensuring a safe and positive learning environment for every child. Built on principles of honesty, hard work, and respect, the school aims to shape responsible citizens along with successful students. Teachers guide children not just in academics but also in building character, teamwork, and leadership qualities.


Career guidance is an important part of the school’s vision. From Class VIII onwards, students are motivated to discover their interests and strengths, preparing them for future studies in science, commerce, or arts. Regular workshops, counseling, and exposure to different fields help them make confident choices for their future. By the time students complete Class X, they leave with strong knowledge, discipline, and values—well-prepared for higher education and for building successful careers


Beyond classroom learning, the school actively promotes values such as respect, teamwork, and responsibility. Regular cultural programs, sports events, and interactive sessions are organized to help students discover their talents and build leadership qualities. By the time they complete Class X, students are well-prepared not only for higher studies but also for the responsibilities of a progressive society.

Principal Message

Our aim is to provide quality education while instilling discipline, values, and a love for learning in every child. We believe that education is not limited to academics alone but also includes building character, confidence, and life skills. At Sri Krishna High School, equal importance is given to studies, sports, and extracurricular activities to ensure the all-round development of students.

We expect our children to follow school rules sincerely, respect teachers, and maintain discipline, as these qualities are the foundation of success. Parents are our valued partners in this journey, and with their support and encouragement, we can help students excel in studies while also preparing them to become responsible, compassionate, and successful individuals in the future. Together, we can make the schooling years a joyful, meaningful, and enriching experience for every child.
