The Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE), conducted by the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE), is a Class 10 examination recognized in India and abroad. The curriculum is known for its balance between academics and co-curricular activities, with a strong focus on language, analytical skills, and practical learning. It emphasizes conceptual understanding over rote memorization and provides students with a wide range of subjects to choose from, ensuring holistic development. Assessment includes both internal evaluations and board exams, encouraging students to develop research and application-based knowledge. The ICSE curriculum is especially valued for its strong foundation in English and its global recognition.

  • Conducted by CISCE for Class 10 students.
  • Balanced curriculum combining academics and co-curricular activities.
  • Emphasis on English language and literature.
  • Subjects grouped into three categories (compulsory, electives, skill-based).
  • Internal assessments (projects, practicals) carry significant weight.
  • Encourages critical thinking, creativity, and application of knowledge.
  • Offers a wide subject choice, including sciences, humanities, arts, and technical subjects.
  • Globally recognized and accepted for higher studies abroad.
