About us

Sri Krishna High School

Our Vision

The vision of Sri Krishna High School is to nurture a generation of students who are not only academically strong but also equipped with values, creativity, and critical thinking skills to thrive in an ever-changing world. The school aspires to provide a balanced education that blends knowledge with character, fostering a spirit of curiosity, discipline, and respect for diversity. By cultivating a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment, Sri Krishna High School envisions its students becoming lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.


Looking toward the future, Sri Krishna High School envisions itself as a center of excellence that inspires innovation, leadership, and social responsibility. The school strives to prepare students to confidently face challenges, embrace opportunities, and contribute positively to society. Through dedicated teaching, holistic development programs, and community engagement, the vision is to empower every learner with the confidence to pursue their dreams while upholding the values of compassion, integrity, and service.

Our Mission

The mission of Sri Krishna High School is to provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their fullest potential in academics, character, and life skills. The school is committed to creating a learner-centered environment where curiosity, creativity, and innovation are encouraged. By integrating modern teaching methods with traditional values, the mission is to ensure that every child receives a well-rounded education that builds confidence, responsibility, and resilience.


Furthermore, Sri Krishna High School aims to instill in students a deep respect for cultural heritage, ethical values, and social responsibility. The mission is to prepare students not only for academic success but also to develop empathy, leadership, and teamwork. Through collaborative efforts between teachers, parents, and the community, the school strives to shape individuals who can adapt to global challenges, contribute positively to society, and uphold the principles of integrity and service throughout their lives.
